Tamales

Description

Tamales with chicken or pork filling

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Pork
  • Flour
  • Lard
  • Canned tomato sauce
  • Corn husks

Steps

  1. Boil chicken and pork
  2. Simmer the boiled chicken and pork with canned tomato sauce
  3. Mix flour with water to make dough
  4. Mix dough and lard
  5. Spread an even layer of dough onto one side of the corn husks using a spoon
  6. Add simmered chicken and pork on the inside of dough covered husks
  7. Wrap them up
  8. Put them into a steamer until done
  9. Optional, let them rest for a day and pan fry them in oil for a crispy exterior
