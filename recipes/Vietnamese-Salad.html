Vietnamese Salad

Description

Noodle salad with a salty, spicy and acidic dressing. It can be made with any protein of your choice, but I prefer beef so that is what I will be using in this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Thinly sliced beef
  • Rice noodles
  • Lettuce
  • Cucumber
  • Lime
  • Lemon grass
  • Bean sprouts
  • Mint
  • Cilantro
  • Butter
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sriracha sauce

Steps

  1. Chop vegetables to your preffered size
  2. Cut beef into thin slices if it did not come pre-sliced
  3. Mix the soy sauce, Sriracha sauce, and lime togehter to make a dressing, how much of each to taste
  4. Cook rice noodles to preffered doneness
  5. Melt butter in a pan, cook beef in butter along with chopped pieces of lemon grass
  6. Assemble everything, my preffered order starting from the bottom is:
    1. Noodles
    2. Beef
    3. Veggies
    4. Dressing
